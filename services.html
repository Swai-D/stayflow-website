What We Offer

Everything Under One Roof

At Buffalo Hotel, we go beyond accommodation. Our full range of services is designed to make your stay in Moshi seamless, enjoyable, and unforgettable — from the moment you arrive to the moment you leave.

Online Room Booking

Reserve your room easily via phone, WhatsApp, or our booking form. Instant confirmation sent via SMS and email. No surprises on arrival.

Kilimanjaro & Safari Tours

We partner with certified, experienced local guides for Kilimanjaro climbs, Serengeti safaris, Ngorongoro Crater day trips, and Zanzibar getaways.

Conference & Meeting Rooms

Modern meeting spaces equipped with projector, whiteboard, and high-speed WiFi. Ideal for workshops, seminars, corporate retreats, and NGO events.

Restaurant & Bar

Savour authentic Tanzanian cuisine including Ugali, Nyama Choma, Pilau, and fresh juices. Our restaurant also serves Continental and international dishes.

Airport Transfers

Reliable and comfortable transport between Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO) and the hotel. Available 24/7 — just let us know your flight details.

Free High-Speed WiFi

Complimentary fast internet throughout the hotel — in all rooms, the restaurant, lobby, and garden. Stay connected wherever you are on the property.

Laundry Services

Same-day laundry service available. Perfect for long-haul travellers and climbers who need fresh, clean gear before or after their Kilimanjaro adventure.

Corporate & Group Stays

Streamlined group booking solutions with dedicated account management, bulk room reservations, and flexible invoicing for NGOs and corporate clients.

24/7 Guest Support

Our front desk team is available around the clock. Need a taxi? Have a question? Emergency? We're always here — day or night — to help.

Restaurant & Bar

Taste the Flavours of East Africa

Freshly prepared Tanzanian meals, local favourites, and international dishes — all enjoyed in a warm, welcoming setting.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and facilities.

What time is check-in and check-out?
Check-in is from 12:00 PM and check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be arranged subject to availability — please contact us in advance.
Is breakfast included in the room rate?
Breakfast is included in Executive Room and Kilimanjaro View Suite packages. For other rooms, breakfast is available in our restaurant at an additional charge. We serve both Tanzanian and Continental options.
Can you arrange Kilimanjaro climb packages?
Yes! We work with licensed, certified Kilimanjaro guides and operators. We can arrange full climb packages including permits, gear rental, guides, porters, and pre/post-climb accommodation. Contact us with your preferred dates for a custom quote.
Is there parking available at the hotel?
Yes, we have secure on-site parking available for guests at no extra charge. The parking area is monitored 24/7 by our security team.
Do you accept international credit cards?
We accept major international credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard) as well as cash in Tanzanian Shillings (TZS), US Dollars, and mobile money via M-Pesa and Tigopesa.
How far is the hotel from Kilimanjaro Airport?
Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO) is approximately 50km from Moshi town — about 45 minutes by road. We offer reliable, pre-booked airport transfers for all guests. Book your transfer when making your room reservation.

Ready to Plan Your Stay?

Our team is here to help you book rooms, arrange tours, or answer any questions.
