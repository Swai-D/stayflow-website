Our Story

Hospitality at the Foot of Kilimanjaro

Buffalo Hotel was founded with one clear purpose: to give every traveller — whether a seasoned adventurer, a business professional, or a curious explorer — a warm, comfortable, and genuinely Tanzanian home in Moshi.

Over the years, we have welcomed guests from all over the world — climbers preparing for Africa's highest peak, NGO workers, families on safari, and solo backpackers. Each one has left with stories, friendships, and memories of a place that felt like home.

We are deeply rooted in the local community of Moshi. Our team is made up of local Tanzanians who take pride in sharing their culture, their food, and their land with every guest who walks through our doors.
